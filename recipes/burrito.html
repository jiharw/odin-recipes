Burrito

Description

This is the chipotle burrito that I order everytime I eat there.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Half white, half brown rice
  • Black beans
  • Chicken
  • Mild salsa
  • Cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Sour Cream
  • Corn

Steps

  1. Warm the Tortilla
  2. Add the rice onto the tortila
  3. Next add the black beans and chicken
  4. Then add your corn, mild salsa, cheese, guac, and sour cream
  5. Wrap up into a burrito
  6. Enjoy
