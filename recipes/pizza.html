Pizza

Description

This is the recipe for how I would would make pizza at home.

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Hot honey
  • Tomato sauce
  • Lots of cheese
  • Seasonings & Cilantro
  • Pizza dough

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza Dough
  2. Add tomato sauce to the dough
  3. Add on pepperoni, lots of cheese
  4. Put in the pizza oven for 5-10 minutes
  5. Once it is fully cooked take it out
  6. Add on your hot honey and other seasonings
  7. Cut your pizza and enjoy
