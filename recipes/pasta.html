Pasta

Description

This is the recipe for how I typically make my pasta at home.

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni noodles
  • Ground turkey
  • Tomato sauce
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Seasonings & Cilantro
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water for the noodles
  2. In a pan add olive oil and the ground turkey
  3. Add seasoning as you cook the ground turkey while stirring for about 7 minutes
  4. Once your pot water is boiling add in the rigatoni noodles and stir every minute or so
  5. Drain the extra oils out of the pan
  6. Pour the alfredo and tomato sauce into the pan with the ground turkey
  7. Mix and add in more seasoning and cilantro
  8. After the noodles have boiled for about 8 minutes pour them out into a strainer
  9. Put the noodles and ground turkey together in a bowl
  10. Enjoy
